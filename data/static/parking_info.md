# CityPark Premium Parking — Complete Information Guide

## Overview

CityPark Premium Parking is a modern, state-of-the-art parking facility located in the Sloterdijk business district of Amsterdam. We offer secure, well-lit, and convenient parking for short-term visitors, daily commuters, and monthly subscribers. Our automated facility operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, ensuring your vehicle is safe at all times.

**Total capacity:** 150 parking spaces across 3 underground levels.

## Parking Levels & Space Types

The facility has three underground levels:

| Level | Type Label | Description                              | Number of Spaces |
|-------|-----------|------------------------------------------|-----------------|
| B1    | Ground     | Easiest access, near elevator and exit   | 50              |
| B2    | Mid-level  | Standard access via ramp or elevator     | 50              |
| B3    | Deep-level | EV charging section and compact spaces   | 50              |

**Space types available:**
- **Standard spaces** (100 spaces): Regular-size spaces for sedans, SUVs, and most vehicles.
- **Compact spaces** (20 spaces): Designed for smaller vehicles. Surcharge-free.
- **Handicapped/Accessible spaces** (10 spaces): Blue-badge holders only, closest to elevators. Valid permit required.
- **EV Charging spaces** (20 spaces): Electric vehicle charging bays on Level B3. CHAdeMO and CCS connectors available.

## Booking Process

### How to Reserve a Parking Space

You can reserve a space directly through this chatbot! The process is simple:

1. **Tell the chatbot you want to reserve a space.** Say something like "I want to book a parking spot" or "reserve a space".
2. **Provide your details when asked:**
   - First name and last name
   - Vehicle registration plate number (e.g. "ABC-1234")
   - Desired start date and time
   - Desired end date and time
3. **Confirm the booking summary.** The chatbot will show you a summary with the estimated cost. Type "yes" to confirm.
4. **Administrator confirmation.** Once submitted, a CityPark administrator will review and confirm your reservation. You will be notified in the chat.

### Modification & Cancellation

- Cancellations made **more than 24 hours** before the start time: No charge.
- Cancellations made **within 24 hours**: 50% of the estimated cost is charged.
- To cancel: Contact us at info@citypark.com or call +31 20 555 0123, quoting your Reservation ID.

### Walk-in Parking

Walk-in parking (without prior reservation) is available subject to space availability. Just drive to the entrance, take a ticket, and pay on exit. No reservation required for walk-ins.

## General Rules & Policies

1. **Maximum vehicle height:** 2.1 metres. Vehicles exceeding this limit cannot enter.
2. **Maximum vehicle weight:** 3,500 kg gross.
3. **Speed limit inside:** 5 km/h on all levels.
4. **No overnight unattended camping** in the vehicle.
5. **Motorcycles** are welcome and use designated motorcycle bays (free of charge with standard reservation).
6. **No hazardous materials**: Vehicles carrying LPG tanks or hazardous cargo are not permitted.
7. **Pets**: Pets must remain inside the vehicle or be on a lead.
8. **Damage liability**: CityPark is not liable for theft or damage unless proven negligence.
9. **Abandoned vehicles**: Vehicles left beyond the reservation period will be subject to additional charges and may be towed after 48 hours.
10. **Blue badge (handicapped)**: Valid permit must be displayed at all times. Misuse will result in removal at owner's cost.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: Can I enter without a reservation?**
A: Yes! Walk-in parking is accepted whenever spaces are available. Just use the barrier at the entrance.

**Q: What happens if I stay longer than my reservation?**
A: Overtime is charged at the standard hourly rate. The barrier will not open until outstanding charges are settled at the payment kiosk.

**Q: Is there a grace period?**
A: Yes, we offer a 15-minute grace period after your reservation end time before overtime charges begin.

**Q: Can I reserve multiple spaces?**
A: Yes, contact us at info@citypark.com for group or corporate reservations.

**Q: Is my car safe overnight?**
A: Absolutely. 24/7 CCTV surveillance, security patrols, and locked access barriers ensure maximum security.

**Q: Do you offer monthly subscriptions?**
A: Yes! Monthly subscriptions give unlimited in/out access. Contact us for a custom quote.

**Q: Are there family-friendly features?**
A: We have spacious family bays (wider than standard) on B1, close to the elevator.

**Q: Is there parking for motorcycles and bicycles?**
A: Yes. Dedicated motorcycle bays are located on B1 (free of charge). There are no bicycle facilities inside the facility.

**Q: What EV connectors are available?**
A: CHAdeMO (50 kW) and CCS Type 2 (up to 100 kW). Check availability via the chatbot or website.

**Q: How do I get a receipt or invoice?**
A: Receipts are printed at the exit kiosk. Invoices can be requested by emailing info@citypark.com with your Reservation ID.

**Q: What should I do in an emergency?**
A: Use the emergency call point (red button, available every 20 metres) or call +31 20 555 0123.
